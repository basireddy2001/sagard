# Investment Memo — GreenLeaf Logistics
**Date:** September 2024
**Round:** Series A, $18M (Sagard: $8.5M for 12%)
**Sector:** Climate / Supply Chain SaaS

## Thesis
GreenLeaf provides scope-3 carbon-tracking for cold-chain logistics operators (grocers, pharma distributors, frozen-foods 3PLs). The CSRD reporting mandate in the EU and the SEC climate-disclosure rule in the US create a hard regulatory deadline driving urgency.

## Market
Cold-chain logistics is a $300B global market with the highest emissions intensity in transport. Regulatory pressure is acute through 2026-2028.
