# FinPay Analytics — Q1 2026 Quarterly Update

## Headline Metrics
- **ARR:** $24.8M, up 80% year over year (vs. $13.8M in Q1 2025)
- **New logo bookings:** $3.1M, slightly below plan ($3.4M)
- **Net revenue retention:** 112% (down from 138% at investment)
- **Gross margin:** 69% (down 3 pts YoY due to increased implementation services)

## Operational Notes
- Revenue grew 80% year over year, driven by mid-market expansion in North America
- Burn rate increased 45% over the prior quarter to $2.0M/month, driven by go-to-market hiring and a marketing push targeting enterprise segment
- Enterprise customer churn increased from 4% to 11% — three logos lost in Q1 (one to PayLogic, two cited "did not realize ROI in expected timeframe")
- Sales cycle expanded from 45 days to 82 days as the company pushed upmarket
- The CFO left the company last quarter to "pursue a new opportunity"; interim CFO is the VP Finance, search underway

## Product Milestones
- Launched new fraud-monitoring module — early traction with two design partners
- Latency improvements: median reconciliation P95 down from 4.1s to 1.8s
- ISO 20022 migration tooling shipped to all customers ahead of EU deadline

## Customer Health
- 28 enterprise customers at end of Q1 (vs. 31 entering Q1)
- Customer reviews on G2 mention implementation delays of 3-5 months for enterprise tier
- Mid-market segment NPS remains strong at 62
