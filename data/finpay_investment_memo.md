# Investment Memo — FinPay Analytics
**Date:** March 2024
**Round:** Series B, $42M (Sagard lead: $18M for 14.5%)
**Sector:** B2B FinTech SaaS — payments analytics

## Thesis
FinPay Analytics provides real-time reconciliation and payments-analytics infrastructure for mid-market enterprises ($100M-$2B revenue) that have outgrown spreadsheet-driven finance ops but cannot justify a full Workday/NetSuite finance-analytics implementation.

## Market
TAM estimated at $14B globally for mid-market payments analytics, growing 28% CAGR. Tailwinds from open-banking regulation in EU/UK and ISO 20022 migration in North America.

## Product
- Real-time payment reconciliation across 40+ payment providers
- AI-driven anomaly detection on payment flows
- Cash forecasting with 92% 30-day accuracy reported by reference customers
- Onboarding typically 30-45 days end-to-end

## Team
- CEO: 2x prior fintech founder, one exit to Visa
- CFO: ex-Stripe FP&A leader (joined Series A)
- CTO: ex-Plaid platform engineering
- 64 FTE at time of investment

## Financials at investment
- ARR: $11M, growing 130% YoY
- Burn: $1.4M/month
- Gross margin: 72%
- Net retention: 138%
- LTV/CAC: 4.6x

## Risks identified at investment
1. Long enterprise sales cycle (initial 45-60 days)
2. Provider concentration — top 3 payment providers account for 58% of routed volume
3. Increasing competition from Modern Treasury and other treasury-management entrants

## Recommendation at time
Lead Series B at $190M post. Strong product-market fit and category leadership in mid-market segment.
