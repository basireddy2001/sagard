# FinPay Analytics — Board Meeting Notes
**Date:** May 12, 2026
**Attending:** Sagard (J. Park), Founder/CEO, Interim CFO, Lead independent director, Series A investor

## Key discussion points

1. **CFO transition.** CEO confirmed the CFO departure was driven by disagreement on the pace of go-to-market investment vs. controls discipline. Board chair raised concerns about financial controls during the search; the audit committee will meet bi-weekly until a permanent CFO is in place. Three candidates currently in final-round interviews.

2. **Churn deep-dive.** Of the three enterprise losses in Q1, two cited a 5+ month implementation timeline as the root cause and one switched to PayLogic. CEO presented a remediation plan: a dedicated implementation pod, milestone-based pricing, and a "white-glove" tier for ARR > $500K.

3. **Burn / runway.** CEO proposed continuing the elevated GTM spend through Q2, then reassessing. Sagard pushed back on the runway profile (~12 months) and asked for a downside-case plan that holds opex flat. Action: CFO and CEO to circulate a base + downside opex plan by next board meeting.

4. **Competitive update.** PayLogic's $90M Series C is being used to fund a U.S. expansion. Initial pricing data suggests they are 15-25% below FinPay in mid-market. CEO believes product depth defends the segment but agreed to commission a competitive-win-rate audit.

5. **Hiring freeze (partial).** GTM hiring continues at plan; engineering hiring paused for Q2. Two roles already on hold.

6. **Risks flagged by independent director:** sustained NRR compression below 110% would trigger renegotiation conversations with at least 4 named accounts ($2.1M ARR at risk).
