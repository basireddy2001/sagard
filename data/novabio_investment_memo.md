# Investment Memo — NovaBio Diagnostics
**Date:** January 2025
**Round:** Series B, $55M (Sagard: $22M for 11%)
**Sector:** HealthTech — molecular diagnostics

## Thesis
NovaBio runs a multiplex molecular diagnostics platform for outpatient clinics (respiratory, GI, women's health panels). Their differentiated wet-lab + cartridge approach delivers 60-minute turnaround at ~30% lower cost than incumbent reference labs.

## Team
- CEO: ex-Roche Dx GM, North America
- CMO: published academic in molecular diagnostics
- 110 FTE

## Financials at investment
- Revenue: $42M, growing 70% YoY
- Gross margin: 58% (improving)
- EBITDA: nearly breakeven
- Cash: $80M post-round

## Risks identified
1. FDA timing on new panel clearances
2. Reimbursement landscape
3. Reference-lab pushback through bundled pricing
